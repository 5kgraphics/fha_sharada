Frequently Asked Questions

What do the Annual Dues cover?

The Annual Dues are used to cover the day-to-day operational costs of the residents' association, including security patrols, street lighting, refuse collection, and administrative expenses. They ensure the smooth running of our community services.

Why is there a Special Levy for roads?

The Special Levy is a one-off contribution dedicated specifically to the ongoing rehabilitation and maintenance of our internal road network. It is a separate fund to ensure our roads remain in good condition and are not impacted by general expenses.

How can I get a receipt for my payment?

Upon successful payment, a digital receipt will be automatically sent to the email address you provide. If you have any issues receiving your receipt, please contact our support team at fhasharada@email.com with your transaction details.
