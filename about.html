About Our Community

Dedicated to fostering a vibrant, secure, and harmonious community for all residents.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for all residents of FHA Sharada by promoting a safe, clean, and well-maintained environment. We strive to foster a strong sense of community, encourage active participation, and ensure effective communication among residents and the association leadership.

We are committed to transparent governance, responsible resource management, and continuous improvement in all aspects of community living.

Our Vision

To be a model residential community in Abuja, recognized for its exceptional living standards, proactive community engagement, and a harmonious environment where every resident feels proud and secure.

We envision a future where FHA Sharada is synonymous with excellence, innovation, and sustainable development, driven by the collective efforts of its residents.

Our Core Values

Community

Fostering strong bonds, mutual respect, and a supportive network among all residents.

Security

Ensuring a safe and secure environment through proactive measures and vigilance.

Innovation

Embracing new ideas and technologies to improve community services and living standards.

Transparency

Operating with openness, honesty, and accountability in all association matters.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally friendly practices and long-term community well-being.

Communication

Maintaining clear, timely, and effective communication channels with all residents.

Our History

The FHA Sharada Residents Association was established in 2008 by a group of visionary residents who recognized the need for a unified body to manage and improve the estate's common facilities and foster community spirit.

Since its inception, the association has been instrumental in numerous developmental projects, including the construction of the Community Hall, upgrading of security infrastructure, and implementation of effective waste management systems. We have grown from a small group of committed individuals to a robust and inclusive organization, continuously striving to meet the evolving needs of our growing community.

Our journey has been marked by dedication, collaboration, and the unwavering support of our residents, making FHA Sharada Phase 1 a truly desirable place to live.
