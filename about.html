Our Community

Welcome to FHA Sharada

FHA Sharada is a vibrant residential community located in Kano, Nigeria. Established in 2008, our community has grown into a thriving neighborhood that values peace, security, and development.

We take pride in our well-maintained infrastructure, beautiful green spaces, and strong sense of community. Our residents enjoy a high quality of life with access to excellent amenities and a supportive neighborhood environment.

The FHA Sharada Residents Association works tirelessly to ensure that our community remains a safe, clean, and enjoyable place for all residents. Through various initiatives and programs, we foster a spirit of cooperation and mutual respect among our diverse residents.
