Essential Safety Tips

Stay Safe in FHA Sharada

  • **Secure Your Home:** Always lock doors and windows, even when stepping out for a short while. Consider extra locks or security bars.
  • **Verify Visitors:** Do not open your gate or door to strangers. Always verify the identity of utility workers or service providers.
  • **Be Vigilant:** Pay attention to your surroundings, especially during early mornings and late evenings. Report any unusual or suspicious persons/vehicles.
  • **Mind Your Information:** Avoid sharing personal routines or travel plans on social media. Be cautious about information shared with new acquaintances.
  • **Maintain Security Systems:** Regularly check and maintain your CCTV, alarm systems, and perimeter fencing. Ensure outdoor lighting is functional.
  • **Know Your Neighbors:** Build good relationships with immediate neighbors. They can be an extra set of eyes when you're away.
  • **Register Domestic Staff:** Ensure all your domestic staff (drivers, nannies, cleaners, etc.) are properly vetted and registered with the Residents Association office as per estate policy.
  • **Avoid Unverified Vendors:** Be wary of hawkers or unverified vendors entering the estate. Patronize approved or known service providers.
