Community Rules and Regulations

  • All residents must pay their monthly dues by the 10th of every month to avoid penalties.
  • Parking is only allowed in designated areas. Street parking that obstructs traffic is prohibited.
  • Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM daily. Please keep noise to a minimum during these hours.
  • All construction or renovation work must be approved by the association and can only be done between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM on weekdays.
  • Proper waste disposal is mandatory. All garbage must be properly bagged and placed in designated bins.
  • Residents are responsible for the conduct of their guests and domestic staff within the estate.
  • No commercial activities are allowed in residential properties without proper approval from the association.
  • All pets must be registered with the association and kept on leash when outside the owner's property.
  • Residents must maintain their property exteriors to community standards, including lawn care and painting.
  • Security gates must not be propped open and all visitors must be properly identified before entry is granted.
  • No burning of waste or any materials within the estate premises.
  • All vehicles must display valid resident parking stickers for identification.
  • Residents must report any security concerns or suspicious activities to the estate security immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay my monthly dues?
Monthly dues can be paid through bank transfer to the association's account or at the monthly general meeting. Our account details are available on the Dues Payment page. Please ensure you include your house number as reference.
What should I do if I notice a security concern?
Immediately contact the estate security through the emergency numbers provided to all residents. You can also report to any executive committee member or use the security update channel on our WhatsApp group.
How can I report a maintenance issue?
Maintenance issues can be reported through the Maintenance Request form available on this website or by contacting the maintenance committee chairperson. For emergencies like water leaks or electrical issues, call the emergency maintenance line.
Are there restrictions on renovations?
Yes, all major renovations must be approved by the association to ensure they comply with community standards. This includes structural changes, exterior modifications, and any work that might disturb neighbors. Submit your plans to the association for review before beginning work.
How do I get a resident parking sticker?
Parking stickers are issued by the security committee. Bring proof of residence (utility bill or tenancy agreement) and vehicle registration documents to the monthly meeting or contact the security chairperson to arrange collection.
